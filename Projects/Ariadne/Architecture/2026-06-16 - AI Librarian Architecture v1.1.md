# AI Librarian Architecture v1.1

**Created:** 16 June 2026

## Overview

This architecture represents the first major revision of the original AI Librarian Architecture.

Unlike Version 1, which was developed through internal design discussions, this revision was produced after the architecture underwent independent review by several advanced language models. Each model analysed the design from its own perspective, identifying strengths, weaknesses and opportunities for improvement.

Rather than accepting the recommendations of any single model, the feedback was compared, evaluated and synthesised into a revised architecture.

The result was a stronger and more balanced design while preserving the original vision of Ariadne as an AI Librarian.

---

## Background

Following completion of the original architecture, the design was deliberately submitted to several independent frontier language models for critique.

Each model focused on different aspects of the system.

Some highlighted architectural separation.

Others questioned governance, evaluation or long-term scalability.

The objective was not to obtain agreement, but to expose blind spots that might otherwise remain unnoticed.

This document records the first architecture that benefited from genuine multi-model peer review.
