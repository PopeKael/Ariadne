# AI Appliance for Small to Medium Business

**Created:** 26 June 2026

## Overview

This group of concept images explores how Project Ariadne could evolve from a personal AI assistant into a practical platform for organisations of every size.

Earlier architectural work concentrated on the AI Librarian itself: how it thinks, routes requests, builds knowledge and maintains context. This stage of the project shifted the focus from the AI to the people who would actually use it every day.

The question became:

> "If every business eventually has its own private AI appliance, what would that experience actually look like?"

Rather than producing another chatbot, the objective became designing a complete working environment where AI is simply another member of the organisation.

---

## Background

Having documented the home laboratory and local AI infrastructure, it became clear that modern hardware was beginning to make private AI deployment realistic.

Instead of assuming every organisation would send confidential information to cloud services, Ariadne explored the opposite direction.

The AI would live inside the organisation.

The knowledge would remain local.

Employees would simply interact with it as naturally as they currently interact with email or their CRM.

---

## The Business Concepts

The accompanying images are not intended as finished software designs.

They are vision boards that demonstrate how the same underlying architecture could appear across different industries.

### Real Estate

Drawing from years of building information systems for commercial organisations, the real estate example demonstrates how Ariadne could become the intelligence layer across listings, clients, calendars, contracts and marketing material.

Instead of searching multiple systems, staff simply ask questions in natural language.

---

### Legal Practice

The legal concept focuses on precedent searching, document retrieval, client history, compliance and matter management.

Law firms rely heavily on institutional knowledge. Ariadne becomes the trusted librarian that understands documents while maintaining privacy and traceability.

---

### Creative Agency

Creative businesses accumulate enormous volumes of briefs, artwork, campaigns, revisions and client conversations.

This concept demonstrates Ariadne acting as the organisational memory, allowing creative staff to spend less time searching for assets and more time creating.

---

### Manufacturing

Manufacturing environments generate operational data from production schedules, engineering drawings, inventory, maintenance records and quality systems.

Here Ariadne becomes the conversational interface across the factory rather than replacing existing production software.

---

### Personal Workspace

The final concept returns to the project's original inspiration.

Rather than designing for a company, it imagines the author's own daily working environment.

Projects.

Research.

Health.

Finance.

Correspondence.

Creative work.

Knowledge management.

Everything appears within one unified workspace driven by the local Knowledge Vault.

This image represents the personal version of the same architecture shown in the business examples.

---

## Design Philosophy

These concepts are intentionally aspirational.

They establish a visual target rather than an implementation plan.

Just as architects produce concept drawings before detailed engineering begins, these images provide a common vision for where Project Ariadne may eventually evolve.

The objective is not to predict the future with precision.

The objective is to build towards a future that is coherent, practical and centred on user sovereignty, privacy and intelligent knowledge management.

---

## Historical Significance

This collection marks an important transition in Project Ariadne.

The project stopped asking,

*"How do we build an AI Librarian?"*

and began asking,

*"How does an AI Librarian fit naturally into everyday life?"*

That shift transformed Ariadne from a software architecture into a vision for an entire working environment.

These images serve as visual milestones documenting that evolution.
