# Ariadne Vision

## Vision

Ariadne is an open architecture for a trusted AI librarian.

Its purpose is not to replace human thought, but to preserve, organise, connect and recall knowledge across a lifetime. Ariadne serves as a persistent intellectual companion that grows alongside its user, remembers decisions, understands context, and helps transform accumulated information into usable wisdom.

Rather than acting as another chatbot, Ariadne acts as a librarian, archivist, researcher and guide. It understands where knowledge lives, how ideas relate to one another, and which intelligence is best suited to answer a particular question.

---

## Mission

To build a platform that allows a person to retain and interact with decades of accumulated knowledge, regardless of which AI models, software platforms or technologies exist in the future.

The knowledge belongs to the user.

The AI serves the knowledge.

---

## Core Principles

### Knowledge First

Knowledge is the permanent asset.

Artificial intelligence is a tool that interprets and organises that knowledge but never owns it.

### Open Standards

All knowledge is stored in human-readable Markdown.

No proprietary database should become a single point of failure.

The knowledge must remain portable for decades.

### AI Agnostic

Ariadne is independent of any single AI provider.

OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, local models, or future systems should all be interchangeable components.

### Memory Over Conversation

Most AI systems remember only the current conversation.

Ariadne remembers projects, research, design decisions, successes, failures and accumulated experience across years.

### Explainable Decisions

Every important architectural decision should be documented.

Future versions of Ariadne should understand not only *what* decisions were made, but *why* they were made.

### Human Partnership

The goal is augmentation rather than automation.

Ariadne assists judgement rather than replacing it.

The human remains the decision maker.

---

## Scope

Ariadne is intended to become a long-term personal knowledge platform capable of:

- Organising research and reference material.
- Building interconnected knowledge graphs.
- Maintaining journals and project histories.
- Recording design decisions.
- Managing personal and professional relationships.
- Assisting creative work.
- Acting as an AI routing and orchestration layer.
- Providing long-term continuity across changing AI technologies.

---

## Long-Term Goal

To create a trusted companion that helps its user think more clearly, remember more accurately, and build upon a lifetime of accumulated knowledge.

Ariadne should become more valuable with age.

Every conversation, project and discovery should strengthen the system rather than disappear into history.
